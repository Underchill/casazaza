Important Guest Information

Property Management & Concierge

Our property manager is available to assist you daily. This listing includes complimentary concierge services to help curate your perfect Costa Rican experience, from booking surf lessons and ATV tours to arranging rental cars, private chefs, and massages.

Security

Nosara is a wonderful community. However, like any tourist area, petty theft can occur. We advise guests to be mindful of their belongings. The guest is responsible for securing valuables and ensuring all security features are enabled when leaving the property.

Jungle Creatures

Welcome to the jungle! While we make every effort to present a well-cleaned home, nature is all around us. You can expect to see ants, beetles, spiders, and other insects. To minimize their presence, please keep food wrapped and stored, and screen doors closed.

Refunds & Insurance

No refunds will be given for natural disasters, utility outages, security breaches, or nearby construction noise. We strongly encourage all guests to purchase Traveler Insurance to protect against unforeseen circumstances.
